Guilherme Alexander

Soundtrack Composer
Guilherme Alexander is a music producer and composer specializing in video game soundtracks, though he also works on projects for film, animation, and more. He holds a degree in composition and conducting, has experience across a wide range of projects, and has completed numerous additional courses since graduating. The website aims to showcase his work to clients, featuring his portfolio, social media links, and contact information.
